Cybersecurity Awareness

Risks in Remote Voting

🎭
Identity Fraud

Impersonation using stolen Voter IDs, deepfake biometrics, and forged credentials are primary threats in remote voting environments.

💻
Cybersecurity Threats

Man-in-the-middle attacks, DDoS on voting servers, SQL injection, and session hijacking can compromise election integrity.

⚠️
Coercion Risks

Remote voting removes ballot secrecy. Voters may be coerced by employers, family, or criminal groups to vote in a certain way.

⚖️ Official Disclaimer

This is a simulation. Real remote voting is not currently implemented in India. The Election Commission of India uses physical EVMs and VVPATs at designated polling booths. This prototype is for educational and research purposes only. No actual votes are cast or recorded.
